# Dashboard

**Route:** `/prioritization/dashboard`

The Dashboard is the first page you see after signing in. It gives you a strategic awareness view of what is happening in the workspace — active cycles, sessions, items needing attention, and quick links into the tools you use most.

## What you see

The layout changes based on whether your workspace has data:

### New or empty workspace
When the workspace is new, the Dashboard shows an onboarding layout with three columns:
- **Getting started checklist** — links to the first actions (add items, set up a cycle, invite teammates)
- **How it works** — a brief explanation of the core Priorities.ai model
- **Prioritization toolkit** — cards for each built-in tool (stack ranking, weighted scoring, Eisenhower matrix, impact/effort, AHP, cost of delay, pyramid, custom matrix)

### Active workspace
Once your workspace has data, the Dashboard switches to a **widget layout** — three columns of live widgets showing the data relevant to you. Examples of what widgets can display:
- Active cycle and its status
- Items that need triage or definition
- Sessions scheduled or in progress
- Scoring gaps or readiness issues
- Tasks assigned to you

## Actions

| Action | How |
|---|---|
| Customize which widgets appear | Click **Customize dashboard** (top right) or go to **Settings → My Account → Customize Dashboard** |
| Open a tool directly | Click any tool card in the toolkit grid |
| Browse all tools | Click **Browse all tools** below the toolkit grid |
| Follow onboarding links | Click any item in the getting-started checklist |

## Tips

- The greeting at the top adapts to the time of day and shows your first name if your profile is complete.
- Widget content is live — it updates as your team adds items, runs sessions, and publishes results.
- On a new workspace, the widget previews explain what will appear once you have data; they are not live until the relevant objects exist.
